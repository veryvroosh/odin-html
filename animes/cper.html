Cyberpunk: Edgerunners

Dreams in Night City are fated to fail. David Martinez, a teen in the slums, strives to achieve his mother's goal for him to rise to the top of Arasaka,
the world's leading security corporation. While his mom struggles, he attends Arasaka Academy, but a clash with a gang causes his world to crumble.

In a fit of rage, he implants Sandevistan cyberware, granting incredible speed, to take revenge, leading to his expulsion and crushing his dreams.

Enter Lucyna Kushinada (Lucy), a data thief who invites David to join forces. Their bond exposes him to Edgerunners, cybernetic mercenaries
who risk their lives for cash. In his quest to survive, David is ready to risk it all.
